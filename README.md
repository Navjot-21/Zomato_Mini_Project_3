# Zomato_Mini_Project_3
# 🍽️ Exploratory Analysis of Restaurant Trends and Customer Preferences using Zomato Dataset

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the **Zomato Restaurant Dataset** to uncover patterns in restaurant distribution, customer preferences, pricing, cuisines, ratings, and online delivery services.

The objective is to derive actionable business insights that can help Zomato and restaurant owners make informed decisions to improve customer satisfaction and business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the structure and quality of the dataset.
* Clean and preprocess the data.
* Perform descriptive statistical analysis.
* Analyze restaurant distribution across cities.
* Identify the most popular and highest-rated cuisines.
* Study customer rating patterns.
* Compare restaurants with and without online delivery.
* Analyze restaurant pricing and customer ratings.
* Perform correlation analysis.
* Create new engineered features for better interpretation.
* Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis

The following analyses were performed:

### 📍 Restaurant Distribution Analysis

* Identified the city with the highest number of restaurants.
* Visualized the top 10 cities by restaurant count.

### 🍜 Cuisine Analysis

* Identified the top 10 most common cuisines.
* Determined the highest-rated cuisines.

### ⭐ Rating Analysis

* Examined the distribution of ratings.
* Calculated skewness.
* Detected outliers using the IQR method.
* Visualized ratings using histograms and box plots.

### 🚚 Online Delivery Analysis

* Compared restaurants with and without online delivery.
* Analyzed average customer ratings based on delivery availability.

### 💰 Cost Analysis

* Calculated the average restaurant cost.
* Identified the most expensive restaurants.
* Studied the relationship between cost and ratings.

### 📉 Correlation Analysis

* Generated a correlation matrix.
* Created a heatmap.
* Identified relationships between numerical variables.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Restaurant activity is concentrated in a few major cities.
* Customer votes have the strongest positive relationship with restaurant ratings.
* Restaurant pricing has very little influence on customer ratings.
* Online delivery has only a minor impact on customer satisfaction.
* Most rated restaurants belong to the **Good** rating category.

---

## ✅ Recommendations

* Encourage customers to submit reviews and ratings.
* Focus on improving food quality and customer experience rather than increasing prices.
* Expand restaurant partnerships in emerging cities with growing demand.
* Promote highly rated cuisines to attract more customers.
* Use customer engagement metrics to improve restaurant visibility.

## 📌 Results

The analysis shows that restaurant success depends more on **customer engagement, food quality, and overall dining experience** than on pricing or online delivery alone. Customer votes exhibit the strongest relationship with ratings, while restaurant cost has only a weak correlation with customer satisfaction.
